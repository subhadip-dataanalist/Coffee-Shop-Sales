# Coffee-Shop-Sales
📊 Key Metrics
Total Sales: $15,789.23
Total Footfall: 3,390 customers
Average Bill per Person: $4.66
Average Orders per Person: 1.46
⏰ Sales Trends
Peak order quantity occurs around 10 AM, indicating strong mid-morning demand.
Sales gradually decline after afternoon hours.
🛍️ Product & Category Insights
Coffee dominates sales with 39% contribution.
Tea (29%) and Bakery (11%) are also significant contributors.
Other categories (chocolate, flavors, beans) contribute smaller shares.
🥤 Order Preferences
Regular (31%) and Large (30%) sizes are most popular.
Small size orders account for only 9%, indicating preference for larger servings.
📍 Store Performance
Astoria generates the highest revenue (~$5,360), followed closely by Hell’s Kitchen and Lower Manhattan.
Footfall is relatively consistent across all locations.
🏆 Top Products
Best-selling items include:
Barista Espresso ($2,030.80)
Brewed Chai Tea ($1,775.15)
Hot Chocolate & Gourmet Coffee also perform strongly
📅 Weekly Trends
Sunday and Saturday see the highest orders, indicating strong weekend demand.
Weekdays show stable but slightly lower performance, with Friday being the lowest.
